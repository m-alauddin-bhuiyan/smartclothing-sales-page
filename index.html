About us

Smart Clothing is a well-known and trusted garments buying agent, exporter, and supplier company.


For over a decade, we've been dedicated to providing top-quality garments globally. Our team, consisting of more than 50 professionals, works tirelessly to ensure our clients' satisfaction.


With a focus on knitwear, woven, and sweaters, we have successfully built relationships with over 70 happy clients. At Smart Clothing, we prioritize delivering on time, offering competitive prices, and maintaining strong partnerships with reliable garment associates.


We are equipped with the latest methods to help our valued American and European buyers, making our exports smoother and more efficient
